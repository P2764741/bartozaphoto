About Me

Welcome to my page! My name is Bartoza, and I am a photography enthusiast who turned his passion into a profession over 6 years ago. My specialization lies in the dynamic world of club photography and the elegant realm of interior photography, where my images pulsate with life and colors. Besides, I passionately and devotedly conduct unique photo sessions for women, couples, and men, each picture narrating a distinctive story.

I'm also a first-year student in Media Production at the prestigious DMU University in Leicester. This education allows me to continuously evolve and refine my skills, blending theory and practice in the field of media and production.

My photographic career is as colorful and international as my photos. I've had the pleasure of working in picturesque Greece as well as in my native Poland, gaining experience, inspiration, and creating unforgettable memories for my clients. I treat every assignment as a new adventure and an opportunity to capture unique moments in extraordinary ways.

I invite you to collaborate and discover with me the magic of photography that turns ordinary moments into unforgettable memories.
